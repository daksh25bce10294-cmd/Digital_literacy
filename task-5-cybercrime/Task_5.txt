Stay Safe Online – UPI & Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

Do’s

Always verify the sender before approving any UPI request or payment.
Read carefully: “Pay” vs “Receive” on UPI apps before entering your PIN.
Enable app lock, device lock, and SMS/email alerts for all transactions.
Use only official apps like bank apps or trusted UPI apps from app stores.
Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history.
Set strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
Keep your phone number, email, and banking details updated with your bank.
Immediately report suspicious transactions to your bank.

 Don’ts

Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone.
Do not click on unknown links or download apps from unofficial sources.
Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown people to receive money.
Do not trust callers claiming to be bank officials without verification.
Never approve a collect request unless you are sure you are paying.
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.
Do not store sensitive banking info in notes or messages on your phone.
Don’t ignore small unauthorized transactions—they can indicate fraud.

 
Report Cybercrime Immediately
