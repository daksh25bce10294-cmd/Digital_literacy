Here is a simple Social Media Do’s and Don’ts checklist you can use in your report:

 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

Do’s

** Think before posting any content online
** Maintain a respectful and positive tone
** Use privacy settings to protect your information
** Share useful and meaningful content
** Verify information before sharing


 Don’ts

** Do not share personal or sensitive information
** Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
** Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
** Avoid clicking on suspicious links
** Do not spread fake news or misinformation
